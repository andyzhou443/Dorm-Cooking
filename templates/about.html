About Dorm Cooking

Dorm Cooking was created as a simple and practical resource to help college students easily learn how to make quick, affordable meals with minimal equipment. Our website offers clear lists of necessary ingredients and tools, step-by-step instructions, and helpful video tutorials to make cooking in a dorm room stress-free and fun. Dorm Cooking was developed as a final project for a Columbia University UI Design course by Andy Zhou, Jonas Yukins, Obiora Okeke, and Eileen Gao. Our goal is to empower students to enjoy homemade meals, even with limited space and resources.
